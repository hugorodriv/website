About me

My name is Hugo. I was born in 2003. I am in the last year of my Bachelor's in Computer Science and Engineering in UPF Barcelona.

Ever since I can remember, I have always loved tinkering with and exploring computing in general. The first big project I undertook was modifying the operating system of my Android phone when I was 10 years old, as the stock one was too limited.
To do so, I had to learn basic hardware functions, how an operating system works, what a kernel is, and how to use the command line. I did this almost weekly for a long time, and thatâ€™s when I decided to dedicate my professional life to this field.

More than 10 years later, I still continue digging deeper into how computers work. This is one of the main reasons I like Linux and other OSS tools and projects, as they allow us to better understand the tools we use every day and expand our computing knowledge.
